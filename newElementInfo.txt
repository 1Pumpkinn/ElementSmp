ElementSmp — Element Abilities & Passives (verified from code)

Notes
- Mana costs are read from config.yml. Defaults: Ability 1 = 50, Ability 2 = 75.
- “Upgrade I” is required for Ability 1; “Upgrade II” is required for Ability 2.
- Trust checks prevent harming trusted players across many abilities.

AIR
Passives
- Slow Falling while sneaking in mid-air (always).
- No fall damage at Upgrade II, except when landing on pointed dripstone.

Abilities
- Air Dash [Cost: 50, Upgrade I]
  • Dash forward (velocity ≈ 2.5× look direction). If not looking down, a small upward boost is applied.
  • While dashing, knocks nearby entities back every few ticks (radius ≈ 3, slight upward push).
- Air Blast [Cost: 75, Upgrade II]
  • Launch upward, steer in air, then slam. On landing, emit a shockwave with expanding cloud ring.
  • Nearby entities are launched outward and upward and take ~2 hearts true damage.

WATER
Passives
- Permanent Conduit Power.
- Loyalty Tridents pull hit targets toward the thrower (Upgrade II).

Abilities
- Water Prison [Cost: 50, Upgrade I]
  • Raycast a target within ~15 blocks and trap them in a drowning bubble for ~3 seconds.
  • Air is reduced each tick; when out of air they take damage. Target’s velocity is zeroed.
- Leeching Trident [Cost: 75, Upgrade II]
  • Throw a trident that becomes a visual display orbiting the hit target (~3 block radius at ~2.5 blocks high).
  • Deals true damage periodically (~0.5 heart per second) while orbiting; plays trident sounds/particles.

FIRE
Passives
- Auto-smelt ore drops.
- Apply Fire Aspect (set on fire ~4s) on hit at Upgrade II.

Abilities
- Scorch [Cost: 50, Upgrade I]
  • Your next hit removes the victim’s Fire Resistance, with flame/smoke feedback.
- Inferno Blast [Cost: 75, Upgrade II]
  • Explosion centered on you; deals ~3.5 hearts true damage in ~6 block radius; ignores armor.

EARTH
Passives
- Hero of the Village.
- Double ore drops at Upgrade II (disabled if Silk Touch is used).
- 10% chance to apply Slowness II for ~3s on hit.

Abilities
- Earth Tunnel [Cost: 50, Upgrade I]
  • Toggle tunneling; breaks a 3×3 tunnel in front of you on a timer for up to ~20s. Press again to cancel.
- Earthquake [Cost: 75, Upgrade II]
  • Shockwave around you with dense ground particles; applies Slowness II and Weakness I to nearby enemies (~5s).

LIFE
Passives
- Crop growth aura around you (~5 block radius; accelerates crops to max age).
- Max health becomes 30 HP (15 hearts) at Upgrade II; reverts when not Life.

Abilities
- Nature’s Eye [Cost: 50, Upgrade I]
  • Reveal nearby living entities through walls (glowing effect) for ~5s, with supportive particles/sounds.
- Entangling Roots [Cost: 75, Upgrade II]
  • Raycast a target within ~15 blocks; sink them slightly below ground while immobilizing for ~5s with frost/root visuals, then return them.

DEATH
Passives
- 10% chance to apply Wither (≈5s) on hit.
- At ≤2 hearts and Upgrade II, gain Invisibility and hide equipment to others; auto-maintained until health rises.

Abilities
- Summon Undead [Cost: 50, Upgrade I]
  • Summon a geared Wither Skeleton that follows you across worlds, attacks non‑trusted players, and lasts ~2 minutes.
- Shadow Step [Cost: 75, Upgrade II]
  • Fire a shadow “projectile” with gravity; teleport to the first collided surface, preserving look direction.

METAL
Passives
- Haste I.
- Arrow damage immunity at Upgrade II.

Abilities
- Metal Dash [Cost: 50, Upgrade I]
  • Dash forward, dealing ~2 hearts true damage and knockback to hit entities.
  • If you land without hitting anyone, you become briefly stunned (movement suppressed) then recover.
- Magnetic Accumulation [Cost: 75, Upgrade II]
  • Tag a target for ~3s; damage you deal to them during this window is accumulated and then released as true damage (≈30% of accumulated, capped at ~8).
  • Default melee/projectile damage is canceled against the tagged target; the final burst applies at the end.

FROST
Passives
- Speed III on ice blocks at Upgrade II (ice, packed/blue/frosted).
- Freeze surface water while sneaking at Upgrade II (creates temporary frosted ice under/near you).

Abilities
- Frost Circle [Cost: 50, Upgrade I]
  • Persistent area around you (~5 block radius, ~10s) that builds Freeze ticks, applies Slowness II, and deals light periodic damage.
- Frost Nova [Cost: 75, Upgrade II]
  • Instant frost burst: damages (~3 hearts), applies freeze, and immediately shockwaves entities outward with flat ring particles and upward launch.
