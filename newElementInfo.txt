ElementSmp — Element Guide

General
- Ability 1 cost: 50
- Ability 2 cost: 75

Air
Passives
- Slow Falling while sneaking
- No fall damage at Upgrade II (except pointed dripstone)
Abilities
- Air Dash [Upgrade I]: Dash forward and knock back nearby entities
- Air Blast [Upgrade II]: Leap and slam; launch nearby entities and deal true damage

Water
Passives
- Conduit Power
- Tridents pull hit targets toward you (Upgrade II)
Abilities
- Water Prison [Upgrade I]: Trap a nearby target in a suffocating bubble for a short time
- Leeching Trident [Upgrade II]: A trident orbits a hit target and deals periodic true damage

Fire
Passives
- Auto-smelt ore drops
- Set targets on fire on hit (Upgrade II)
Abilities
- Scorch [Upgrade I]: Your next hit removes Fire Resistance
- Inferno Blast [Upgrade II]: Explosion around you that deals true damage

Earth
Passives
- Hero of the Village
- Double ore drops (Upgrade II; no Silk Touch)
Abilities
- Earth Tunnel [Upgrade I]: Create a short 3×3 tunnel ahead of you
- Earthquake [Upgrade II]: Shockwave that slows and weakens nearby enemies

Life
Passives
- Crop growth aura around you
- Increased max health at Upgrade II
Abilities
- Nature’s Eye [Upgrade I]: Highlight nearby creatures through walls for a short duration
- Entangling Roots [Upgrade II]: Immobilize a nearby target briefly

Death
Passives
- Chance to apply Wither on hit
- Become invisible at low health (Upgrade II)
Abilities
- Summon Undead [Upgrade I]: Summon a Wither Skeleton ally for a short time
- Shadow Step [Upgrade II]: Teleport to the surface your shadow reaches

Metal
Passives
- Haste
- Arrow damage immunity (Upgrade II)
Abilities
- Metal Dash [Upgrade I]: Dash forward, damaging and knocking back what you hit; missing briefly stuns you
- Magnetic Accumulation [Upgrade II]: Tag a target; your damage is stored then released as a burst

Frost
Passives
- Speed boost on ice (Upgrade II)
- Freeze nearby surface water while sneaking (Upgrade II)
Abilities
- Frost Circle [Upgrade I]: Chilling area around you that slows and harms nearby enemies over time
- Frost Nova [Upgrade II]: Instant frost blast that damages and launches nearby enemies
